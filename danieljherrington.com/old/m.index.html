Daniel J Herrington

Background

A graduate of Mississippi State University in electrical engineering, Daniel began his engineering career in FPGA design for Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) as a federal contractor at Redstone Arsenal. In his current role, he operates as a project consultant and design lead for a variety of organaztions throughout the region in both automated test/manufacturing and engineering design including military, aero, medical, and wireless segments.
